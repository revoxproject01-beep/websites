About Global Aid Foundation

Empowering communities through sustainable development and humanitarian assistance

Our Purpose

Understanding what drives us and what we stand for

Our Mission

To empower communities through sustainable development programs that address root causes of poverty and inequality.

We work directly with local partners to implement solutions that create lasting positive change.

Our Vision

A world where every person has access to basic necessities, education, healthcare, and opportunities to thrive.

We envision communities that are self-sufficient, resilient, and able to create their own sustainable futures.

Our Core Values

Compassion

We approach our work with empathy and genuine care for those we serve.

Integrity

We maintain the highest ethical standards in all our operations.

Community

We believe in the power of communities to create their own solutions.

Our Journey

A timeline of milestones in our organization's history

2000

Foundation

Global Aid Foundation was founded by Dr. Sarah Johnson with a mission to provide clean water to underserved communities.

2005

First International Program

We expanded our operations to five countries in Africa, establishing our first international offices.

2010

Education Initiative Launch

We launched our education program, building schools and training teachers in rural communities.

2017

Healthcare Expansion

Our healthcare program expanded to provide mobile clinics and training for local healthcare workers.

2025

Global Reach

Today, we operate in over 140 countries with programs touching the lives of more than 3 million people annually.
