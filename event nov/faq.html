Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about EventSphere 2025

General Information

EventSphere 2025 is a premier global event platform featuring industry leaders, innovative sessions, and networking opportunities. It covers AI, blockchain, cloud computing, cybersecurity, and more.

The conference takes place December 5-7, 2025, at the Global Convention Center, located at 888 8th Avenue, New York, NY 10018.

The conference is perfect for software developers, technology executives, product managers, entrepreneurs, startup founders, and anyone interested in the latest technology trends.

Registration & Tickets

You can register online through our registration page. Simply select your ticket type, fill in your details, and complete the payment process.

We offer three ticket types: Standard ($399), Premium ($599), and Enterprise ($1199). Each includes different levels of access and benefits.

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. 50% refunds are available up to 14 days before the event. No refunds after that date.

Event Details

Topics include Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Blockchain, Cloud Computing, Cybersecurity, Mobile Development, IoT, and Data Science.

Yes, all ticket types include lunch and coffee breaks. Premium and Enterprise tickets also include access to exclusive networking dinners.

Selected sessions will be recorded and made available to registered attendees within 7 days after the conference.

Logistics & Travel

Yes, paid parking is available at the venue. We also recommend public transportation as the venue is easily accessible by subway and bus.

We have partnerships with several nearby hotels offering discounted rates for conference attendees. Check your registration confirmation email for booking codes.

Bring your laptop, business cards, comfortable shoes, and your ticket confirmation. We'll provide all conference materials and WiFi access.
