Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our legal services and consultation process.

How much does a consultation cost?

Our initial consultation is free of charge for potential clients. We believe in providing transparent pricing and will discuss fees during your consultation.

What should I bring to my consultation?

Bring any relevant documents related to your case, such as contracts, correspondence, or court documents. If you're unsure, our intake team can guide you before your appointment.

How long does a consultation last?

Initial consultations typically last 30-60 minutes, depending on the complexity of your case. We ensure sufficient time to address your concerns and provide preliminary guidance.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for qualified clients. Our financial team can discuss options during your consultation to make our services accessible.
