Placing an order on ShopHub is simple and secure:

  1. Browse our product catalog or use the search bar to find what you need
  2. Click on a product to view details, then select quantity and options
  3. Click "Add to Cart" and continue shopping or proceed to checkout
  4. Review your cart and click "Proceed to Checkout"
  5. Enter your shipping information and select a delivery method
  6. Choose your preferred payment method and complete the transaction
  7. You'll receive an order confirmation email with tracking information

Tip: Create an account to save your shipping information for faster checkout in the future.

We offer multiple secure payment options to suit your preferences:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover
  • Digital Wallets: PayPal, Google Pay, Apple Pay, Samsung Pay
  • Bank Transfers: Direct bank transfers for select countries
  • Buy Now, Pay Later: Klarna, Afterpay (where available)

All transactions are protected with 256-bit SSL encryption and processed through secure payment gateways. Your financial information is never stored on our servers.

Shipping times vary based on your location and the shipping method selected:

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. You'll receive tracking information via email once your order ships. Shipping to PO Boxes or APO/FPO addresses may take additional time.

Note: Delivery times may be extended during holidays or peak shopping seasons.

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase:

  • 30-Day Window: Return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund
  • Condition: Items must be unused, in original packaging with all accessories
  • Process: Contact our support team to initiate a return and receive a prepaid shipping label
  • Refund: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the item

Exceptions: Certain items like personalized products, opened software, or hygiene-sensitive items may not be eligible for return. Final sale items are marked clearly at checkout.

Tracking your order is easy with multiple options:

  1. Email Notifications: Receive automatic updates at each stage of your order
  2. Account Dashboard: Log in to view order status and tracking information
  3. Tracking Number: Click the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
  4. Customer Support: Contact us with your order number for immediate assistance

Tracking information typically becomes available 1-2 business days after your order ships. If you haven't received tracking information within 3 business days of your order confirmation, please contact our support team.

Pro Tip: Enable order notifications in your account settings to receive real-time updates via SMS or email.

Order changes depend on the processing stage:

Before Shipment (Within 2 hours)

You can cancel or modify your order by contacting customer support with your order number. Changes to shipping address, items, or payment method are possible during this window.

After Shipment (Processing)

Once an order is in the shipping process, modifications are not possible. You may return items following our standard return policy after delivery.

Subscription Orders

Modify or cancel subscription orders anytime through your account dashboard. Changes take effect before the next billing cycle.

For the fastest assistance, contact our support team immediately after placing your order if you need changes. Include your order number and specific requests in your communication.
