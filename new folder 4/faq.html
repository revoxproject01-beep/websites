Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our restaurant and services

General Questions

We are open:

  • Monday - Thursday: 11:00 AM - 10:00 PM
  • Friday - Saturday: 11:00 AM - 11:00 PM
  • Sunday: 12:00 PM - 9:00 PM

Our kitchen closes 30 minutes before closing time.

Yes, we have a variety of vegetarian and vegan options on our menu. Our chefs are happy to accommodate dietary restrictions and can modify dishes to suit your needs. Please inform your server of any allergies or dietary preferences when ordering.

Yes, we highly recommend making a reservation, especially for dinner and weekend visits. You can:

Ordering & Delivery

Yes, we offer delivery through our own service and partner with major delivery platforms. Delivery is available within a 5-mile radius of our restaurant. Delivery fees apply based on distance.

Our delivery hours are the same as our restaurant hours.

We take food allergies seriously. Please inform your server of any allergies when ordering. Our kitchen staff is trained to handle allergen concerns, but we cannot guarantee against cross-contamination.

Common allergens in our dishes include dairy, eggs, gluten, nuts, and shellfish. Detailed ingredient lists are available upon request.

Yes, we are happy to accommodate special requests. You can:

  • Modify ingredients in most dishes
  • Request specific cooking preparations
  • Adjust spice levels
  • Substitute sides (some substitutions may incur additional charges)

Please note that some modifications may affect the price of your dish.

Events & Catering

Yes, we have private dining rooms that can accommodate events from 10 to 50 guests. We offer:

  • Birthday parties
  • Corporate meetings
  • Wedding receptions
  • Anniversary celebrations

Our event coordinator can help you plan a memorable experience with customized menus and arrangements.

Yes, we provide catering for events of all sizes. Our catering services include:

  • Plated meals
  • Buffet-style service
  • Passed appetizers
  • Full-service event planning

We require at least 48 hours' notice for catering orders. Contact our events team for more information and pricing.
