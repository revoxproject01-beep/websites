How I work

I offer a comprehensive range of digital services including web design and development, mobile app development, UI/UX design, e-commerce solutions, and digital marketing consultation. I specialize in creating modern, responsive websites and applications using the latest technologies.

You can reach me through multiple channels: email at hello@nexusdigital.com, phone at +1 (555) 123-4567, or by filling out the contact form on my website. I typically respond within 24 hours during business days.

I work with both! I can redesign and improve existing websites, add new features, optimize performance, or create completely new websites from scratch. I'll assess your current situation and recommend the best approach for your specific needs and budget.

Yes! I specialize in cross-platform mobile app development using React Native and Flutter. This approach allows me to create apps that work on both iOS and Android while maintaining high performance and native-like user experience.

I offer both project-based pricing and hourly rates depending on the scope of work. For most projects, I prefer fixed pricing as it provides clarity for both parties. I provide detailed quotes after understanding your requirements during our initial consultation.

I typically work with a 50% deposit to start the project and the remaining 50% upon completion. For larger projects, I can arrange milestone-based payments to make it more manageable. Payment terms are always discussed and agreed upon before work begins.

Timeline varies based on project complexity. A simple website typically takes 2-3 weeks, while more complex web applications or e-commerce sites can take 6-12 weeks. I provide realistic timelines during the planning phase and keep you updated throughout the process.

The number of revisions depends on the selected package. Basic projects include 2 revisions, Professional includes 5, and Enterprise includes unlimited revisions. Additional revisions beyond the package limit can be purchased separately.

Yes, I offer various maintenance and support packages. This includes regular updates, security monitoring, bug fixes, and technical support. I believe in long-term relationships and want to ensure your website continues to perform optimally.

I use modern, industry-standard technologies including React, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, and various databases. For mobile development, I use React Native and Flutter. I choose the best technology stack based on your specific needs and project requirements.
