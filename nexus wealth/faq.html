Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our wealth management services

General Questions

We offer a comprehensive range of wealth management services including AI-powered tax planning and preparation, digital accounting and bookkeeping, blockchain auditing and compliance, investment advisory, business advisory services, and financial education. Our services are tailored to meet the specific needs of individuals, startups, small businesses, and large corporations.

Getting started is simple. You can contact us through our website, call us directly, or visit our office. We'll schedule a complimentary consultation to understand your financial needs and goals. Based on this discussion, we'll propose a customized wealth management plan tailored to your requirements.

Our differentiation lies in our AI-powered approach, personalized service, and deep industry expertise. We combine cutting-edge technology with human expertise to deliver exceptional results. Our team of certified professionals takes time to understand your unique situation and provides customized solutions that align with your wealth growth goals.

Account & Support

After signing up for our services, you'll receive login credentials for your personalized dashboard. You can access it by clicking the "Login" button in the top right corner of our website. If you've forgotten your password, you can reset it using the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.

We offer multiple support channels including email, phone, and live chat during business hours. Our clients also have access to our knowledge base and community forums. Premium support is available for Enterprise plan subscribers, including priority response times and dedicated wealth advisors.

The frequency of financial reports depends on your service package. Starter plan subscribers receive monthly reports, Professional plan subscribers get quarterly detailed analyses, and Enterprise plan subscribers receive monthly comprehensive reports with additional insights. Custom reporting schedules can be arranged based on your wealth management needs.

Policies & Data Security

We implement bank-level security measures to protect your financial data. This includes 256-bit encryption, multi-factor authentication, regular security audits, and compliance with financial industry regulations. All data is stored in secure, SOC 2 compliant data centers with 24/7 monitoring.

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all our service plans. If you're not satisfied with our services within the first 30 days, you can request a full refund with no questions asked. After 30 days, refunds are prorated based on the remaining time in your billing cycle.

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no cancellation fees. We believe in earning your business every month. Simply contact our support team to process your cancellation, and you'll continue to have access to our services until the end of your current billing period.
