    General Questions

    NexusTech is a leading IT solutions provider specializing in web development, mobile applications, cloud services, cybersecurity, and digital transformation. We help businesses leverage technology to achieve their goals and stay competitive in the digital landscape.

    NexusTech was founded in 2015 and has been providing innovative IT solutions for over 8 years. Our team has extensive experience in delivering high-quality technology services to clients across various industries.

    We work with businesses of all sizes, from startups to large enterprises. Our solutions are scalable and customizable to meet the specific needs and budgets of each client, regardless of their size.

    Services

    We specialize in modern web technologies including React, Angular, Vue.js for frontend development, and Node.js, Python (Django/Flask), PHP (Laravel), and Ruby on Rails for backend development. We also work with content management systems like WordPress, Drupal, and custom solutions.

    Yes, we develop both native and cross-platform mobile applications for iOS and Android. We use technologies like React Native, Flutter, Swift, and Kotlin to create high-performance, user-friendly mobile experiences.

    Security is integrated into every phase of our development process. We follow industry best practices including secure coding standards, regular security audits, penetration testing, encryption, and compliance with relevant regulations like GDPR and HIPAA.

    Billing

    We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), bank transfers, PayPal, and Stripe. For enterprise clients, we can also arrange custom payment plans and invoicing.

    We offer competitive pricing and occasionally run promotional campaigns. Long-term contracts and bulk services may qualify for volume discounts. Contact our sales team for current offers and custom pricing options.

    Technical

    We follow an agile development methodology with iterative cycles, regular client feedback, and continuous improvement. Our process includes discovery, planning, design, development, testing, deployment, and ongoing maintenance and support.

    We use project management tools like Jira, Trello, and Asana to track progress and maintain transparency. Communication is handled through regular meetings, status reports, and dedicated project managers who serve as your single point of contact.

    Security

    We implement comprehensive data protection measures including encryption at rest and in transit, secure access controls, regular security audits, and compliance with industry standards like ISO 27001 and SOC 2. All team members sign confidentiality agreements.

    Yes, we perform automated daily backups for all client projects with retention policies that vary based on client requirements. Backups are stored in secure, geographically distributed locations to ensure data availability and disaster recovery.

    Support

    We offer comprehensive post-launch support including bug fixes, performance optimization, security updates, and feature enhancements. Support packages are available for ongoing maintenance and can be customized based on your needs.

    Response times depend on your support plan: Critical issues are addressed within 30 minutes, high-priority issues within 2 hours, medium-priority within 8 hours, and low-priority within 24 hours. Emergency support is available 24/7 for critical system failures.
